Florida has its own privacy law that took effect in 2024. If you:
- Have Florida residents as users, OR
- Are based in Florida and process personal data

**Key Requirements:**
- Right to know what data you collect
- Right to access personal information
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of sale of data (you don't sell, so this is easy)
- Right to opt-out of targeted advertising
- Right to opt-out of profiling
