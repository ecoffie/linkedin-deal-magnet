## Competitor Pricing Analysis

### Free Tools (Lead Magnets)

| Tool | Free Tier | Conversion Path |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Careerflow** | Full LinkedIn optimizer, profile score 0-100, section tips | Premium unlocks AI generators ($TBD) |
| **Resume Worded** | Basic resume score, LinkedIn feedback, 250 bullet examples | Pro at $19-29/mo |
| **Teal HQ** | Resume builder, 1 free resume, job tracker | Teal+ at ~$9/week ($36/mo) |
| **Jobscan** | 5 scans/month | $49.95/mo or $29.98/mo (quarterly) |

**Key Insight**: Careerflow gives away the ENTIRE LinkedIn optimizer for free. This is our biggest competitor threat - we can't charge for what they give away.

| **Teal+** | $9/week (~$36/mo) | Subscription | Unlimited AI, match scores, tailored resumes |
| **LinkedIn Premium Career** | $29.99/mo | Subscription | Top applicant badge, InMail, insights |

## Conversion Rate Benchmarks

### Freemium Models
- **Average**: 2-5% free-to-paid conversion
- **Top performers**: 5-10%
- **Industry average for career tools**: 3-5%

### Free Trial Models
- **Average**: 10-25% trial-to-paid
- **With credit card upfront**: 30-50%
- **Career/HR tools specifically**: 22.7%

### Key Formula
> Give away ~80% of functionality free, reserve 20% high-value features for paid

## What Job Seekers Actually Pay For

### High Willingness to Pay

1. **ATS Optimization** - "Will my resume pass the bots?"
   - AI keyword matching to specific job descriptions
   - Resume scoring against job postings

2. **Enhanced Visibility** - "How do I stand out?"
   - LinkedIn "Top Applicant" features
   - Profile optimization for recruiter searches

3. **Curated Access** - "Save me time"
   - Exclusive/vetted job listings (FlexJobs charges $24/mo)
   - Early access to postings

4. **Time Savings** - The #1 value prop
   - AI-generated cover letters
   - One-click application autofill
   - Tailored resume variants

### Low Willingness to Pay

- Generic job listings (perceived as free commodity)
- Vague "optimization" promises
- Features without clear ROI

### Job Seeker AI Adoption (2024-2025)
- 38.2% use AI for most job applications
- 50.6% plan to use AI for resume improvement
- 37.7% will use AI for mock interview practice

## Pricing Psychology Insights

### Charm Pricing Works
- $29 converts better than $30
- $9 outperforms $10
- $49 beats $50

### Price Point Testing
- One founder raised from $9 to $49/mo - **same conversion rate**
- Higher prices = higher perceived value
- Users more invested when they pay more

### Optimal Tier Structure
```
Starter:      $29/mo (limited features)
Professional: $79/mo (most popular - "Goldilocks")
Enterprise:   $199/mo (premium)
```
~70% of customers choose the middle tier.

### One-Time vs Subscription
- One-time: Higher upfront, no commitment anxiety
- Subscription: Lower barrier, but commitment fear
- Hybrid works: One-time for "done" services, subscription for ongoing tools

## Competitive Threats

### Careerflow (Biggest Threat)
- **Free**: Full LinkedIn optimizer with score, tips, walkthrough
- **Chrome extension**: 4.9 rating, easy to use
- **Result**: Users report 2.5X improvement in search appearances
- **Risk**: Why would anyone pay us for what Careerflow gives free?

### Teal HQ (Rising Competitor)
- **Free**: Resume builder, LinkedIn import, job tracker
- **Paid**: Only $9/week for unlimited AI
- **Strength**: All-in-one platform, excellent UX
- **Risk**: Low price point undercuts premium positioning

### Justin Welsh Course (Different Category)
- **Price**: $150 one-time, lifetime access
- **Students**: 15,000+
- **Model**: Video education, not SaaS tool
- **Opportunity**: We could bundle tool + education

## Implications for Our MVP

### Problem: Our Original $97 One-Time Idea

**Issues identified:**
1. Careerflow gives LinkedIn audit FREE - we can't charge for this alone
2. One-time payment doesn't create recurring revenue
3. $97 is awkward - not cheap enough to impulse buy, not premium enough for perceived value
4. "Profile audit" alone isn't enough - need ongoing value

### What Actually Works (Data-Backed)

1. **Free tier must be genuinely valuable** (80% of value)
   - Full profile audit with score ✓
   - AI headline suggestion ✓
   - Top 5-10 fixes ✓

2. **Paid tier needs ONGOING value** (not just more fixes)
   - Resume tailoring per job application
   - LinkedIn content generation
   - Application tracking
   - Ongoing re-audits

3. **Price points that convert**
   - Weekly: $9/week (Teal model) - low commitment
   - Monthly: $19-29/mo (Resume Worded model) - standard SaaS
   - Annual: $99-149/year (discount for commitment)

## Revised Pricing Recommendation

### Option A: Freemium + Subscription (Safest)

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Profile audit, score, headline, top 10 fixes, 1 audit/month |
| **Pro** | $19/mo or $149/year | Unlimited audits, full 25+ fixes, content generator, resume tailoring |
| **Teams** | $49/mo | Multiple profiles, team dashboard (future) |

**Conversion target**: 3-5% free-to-paid

### Option B: Freemium + One-Time + Subscription (Hybrid)

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Profile audit, score, top 5 fixes |
| **Full Report** | $29 one-time | Complete 25+ fixes, PDF export, headline + about rewrite |
| **Pro** | $9/week or $29/mo | Ongoing tools: content generator, resume tailoring, unlimited audits |

**Rationale**: Captures impulse buyers ($29) AND recurring revenue (Pro)

### Option C: Course + Tool Bundle (Justin Welsh Model)

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Profile audit + score |
| **LinkedIn Mastery** | $97-147 one-time | Video course + tool access for 1 year |
| **Pro** | $29/mo | Ongoing tool access after year 1 |

**Rationale**: Higher perceived value, education + tool combo

## Recommended MVP Strategy (Data-Backed)

### Phase 1A: Validate with Free Tool
1. Launch free LinkedIn audit (compete with Careerflow)
2. Capture emails on every audit
3. Track: completion rate, email capture rate, NPS

### Phase 1B: Test Paid Conversion
1. Add "Pro" tier at $19/mo
2. Gate: unlimited audits, content generator, full fixes
3. Track: free-to-paid conversion (target: 3-5%)

### Phase 1C: Optimize Pricing
1. A/B test: $19/mo vs $29/mo vs $9/week
2. A/B test: one-time $29 vs subscription only
3. Double down on winner

### Success Metrics
- Free audit completions: 1,000 in month 1
- Email capture rate: 70%+
- Free-to-paid conversion: 3-5% (30-50 paid users)
- Monthly revenue target: $570-950 (at $19/mo)
