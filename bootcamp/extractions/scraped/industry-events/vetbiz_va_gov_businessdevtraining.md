# Business Readiness Training

OSDBU’s training provide SDVOSB, VOSB, and other small businesses with guidance and resources to enhance their business acumen and procurement readiness.

## Business Readiness Training

OSDBU provides business development training on a variety of topics. This training focuses on providing essential skills and knowledge needed to effectively compete for and secure Federal contracts. The training covers a range of topics such as obtaining the GSA Multiple Award Contract, preparing a proposal, and developing teaming strategies. Course offerings are tailored to accommodate Federal contractors with various levels of knowledge and experience, ensuring that both new entrants and seasoned contractors can benefit from the training. By equipping contractors with the necessary tools and strategies, the training aims to enhance their procurement-readiness. For upcoming training, please visit the [OSDBU events calendar](https://vetbiz.va.gov/businessdevtraining/~/events/). For archived training, please visit the [OSDBU YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/c/VAOSDBU).
