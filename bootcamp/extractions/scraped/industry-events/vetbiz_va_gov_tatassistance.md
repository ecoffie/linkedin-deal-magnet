# Training and Technical Assistance

OSDBU’s training and technical assistance are intended to provide SDVOSB, VOSB, and other small businesses with guidance and resources to become procurement ready.

## Business Readiness Training

[VISIT](https://vetbiz.va.gov/businessdevtraining/ "Visit Business Development Training")

This training focuses on building essential skills and knowledge needed to effectively compete for and secure Federal contracts.

## Direct Access Program (DAP)

[VISIT](https://vetbiz.va.gov/dap/ "Visit Direct Access Program")

DAP offers opportunities to directly interact with procurement decision makers.

## Technical Assistance

[VISIT](https://vetbiz.va.gov/technicalassistance/ "Visit Technical Assistanc")

OSDBU provides targeted, hands-on support for small businesses to enhance their participation in the Federal marketplace
