### Pain Point 1: Student Loan Servicing System Modernization
- **Description**: Legacy student loan servicing systems requiring modernization to handle increased volume and improve borrower experience. Current systems struggle with loan processing, repayment management, and borrower communication.
- **Source**: Department of Education Strategic Plan & Budget Justification FY2025
- **Priority**: high
- **Justification**: Critical operational systems affecting millions of borrowers
- **Evidence**: Student loan portfolio management and servicing modernization initiatives mentioned in budget requests

---

### Pain Point 2: FAFSA System Improvements and Processing Capacity
- **Description**: Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) system requires improvements to handle application volume, reduce processing delays, and enhance user experience for students and families.
- **Source**: Department of Education IT Modernization Initiatives 2025
- **Priority**: high
- **Justification**: Affects millions of students annually, critical for financial aid distribution
- **Evidence**: FAFSA simplification initiatives and IT modernization priorities

---

### Pain Point 3: Legacy IT Systems and Infrastructure Modernization
- **Description**: Aging IT infrastructure and legacy systems across Department operations requiring modernization to improve efficiency, security, and user experience. Multiple systems are outdated and require replacement or significant upgrades.
- **Source**: Department of Education IT Modernization Strategy
- **Priority**: high
- **Justification**: Affects all department operations, security concerns with legacy systems
- **Evidence**: IT modernization budget requests and strategic planning documents

---

### Pain Point 4: Cybersecurity and Student Data Protection (FERPA Compliance)
- **Description**: Ensuring compliance with Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) while maintaining robust cybersecurity measures to protect sensitive student and financial aid data from breaches and unauthorized access.
- **Source**: Department of Education Cybersecurity Strategy & OIG Reports
- **Priority**: critical
- **Justification**: Legal compliance requirement, sensitive data protection
- **Evidence**: FERPA compliance requirements and cybersecurity budget allocations

---

### Pain Point 5: Financial Aid Processing and Grant Management Systems
- **Description**: Systems for processing financial aid applications, managing grant programs, and distributing funds require modernization to improve accuracy, reduce processing time, and enhance oversight capabilities.
- **Source**: Department of Education Budget Justification FY2025
- **Priority**: high
- **Justification**: Core mission function, affects billions in financial aid distribution
- **Evidence**: Grant management system improvements mentioned in budget requests

---

### Pain Point 6: Data Analytics and Reporting Capabilities
- **Description**: Need for improved data analytics, reporting, and business intelligence capabilities to support evidence-based decision making, program evaluation, and performance monitoring across education programs.
- **Source**: Department of Education Strategic Plan
- **Priority**: medium
- **Justification**: Important for program effectiveness but not as urgent as core systems
- **Evidence**: Data-driven decision making priorities in strategic plans

---

### Pain Point 7: Integration and Interoperability Challenges
- **Description**: Multiple disparate systems across the Department require better integration and interoperability to enable data sharing, reduce redundancy, and improve operational efficiency.
- **Source**: Department of Education IT Modernization Initiatives
- **Priority**: medium
- **Justification**: Efficiency improvement, important but not critical
- **Evidence**: System integration initiatives in modernization plans
