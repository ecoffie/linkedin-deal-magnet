### 1. BIOSECURE Act Implementation ‚ö†Ô∏è **NEW COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENT**

**What It Is:**
- Prohibits federal agencies and contractors from using biotechnology equipment or services from companies owned/controlled by foreign adversary governments
- Targets "biotechnology companies of concern" from China and other adversaries
- Affects procurement, research, and operations

**Affected Agencies:**
- ‚úÖ **Department of Health and Human Services** (all components)
- ‚úÖ **NIH** - Research equipment and services

- ‚úÖ **Defense Health Agency** - Medical equipment and biotech systems
- ‚úÖ **Department of Defense** (all components using biotech)
- ‚úÖ **Department of Energy** - Biotechnology research programs

**New Pain Points to Add:**
- BIOSECURE Act compliance - vetting biotechnology suppliers and equipment
- Supply chain security for biotechnology equipment and services
- Replacement of prohibited biotechnology systems and equipment
- Alternative supplier identification for critical biotech capabilities
- Compliance monitoring and reporting for BIOSECURE Act requirements
- Risk assessment for existing biotechnology equipment and services

**Contracting Impact:**
- New procurement restrictions on biotechnology equipment
- Need for supplier vetting and compliance systems
- Potential equipment replacement contracts
- Compliance consulting and assessment services

### 2. Department-Wide AI/ML Security Policy (180-Day Mandate) ‚ö†Ô∏è **URGENT**

**What It Is:**

**Affected Agencies:**
- ‚úÖ **All DoD Components** (mandatory)

- ‚úÖ **Missile Defense Agency (MDA)** - AI for threat detection
- ‚úÖ **All Military Services** - AI integration in operations

**New Pain Points to Add:**
- AI/ML security policy implementation (180-day deadline)
- Model tampering and prompt injection protection
- AI lifecycle security and governance frameworks
- AI testing standards and validation procedures
- AI workforce training and certification programs
- Procurement security framework for AI systems (NIST/CMMC alignment)
- AI risk assessment and threat modeling
- Secure AI development and deployment pipelines

**Contracting Impact:**
- AI security consulting and implementation services
- AI testing and validation tools
- AI governance and compliance systems
- AI workforce training programs
- Secure AI development platforms

### 3. Progressive Design-Build Authorization ‚ö†Ô∏è **NEW PROCUREMENT METHOD**

**What It Is:**
- Codifies use of progressive design-build contracts in DoD
- Allows earlier contractor and designer involvement in complex projects
- Qualifications-based selection with phased price development
- Particularly beneficial for shipyard work, industrial facilities, and complex infrastructure

**Affected Agencies:**
- ‚úÖ **U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE)** - Civil Works and MILCON
- ‚úÖ **NAVFAC** - Naval facilities and infrastructure
- ‚úÖ **Air Force Civil Engineer Center** - Base infrastructure
- ‚úÖ **Defense Logistics Agency (DLA)** - Distribution centers
- ‚úÖ **All DoD Components** - Complex construction projects

**New Pain Points to Add:**
- Progressive design-build contract implementation and management
- Qualifications-based selection processes for design-build teams
- Phased price development and cost control in progressive design-build
- Early contractor involvement in design development
- Design-build team integration and collaboration tools
- Risk management for progressive design-build projects
- Training for acquisition workforce on progressive design-build methods

**Contracting Impact:**
- Design-build team formation and management
- Progressive design-build consulting services
- Project management tools for phased design-build
- Training programs for acquisition professionals

**New Pain Points to Add:**
- Small UAS industrial base capacity expansion (SkyFoundry Program)

- UAS technology development and integration
- Counter-UAS (C-UAS) capabilities for base defense
- UAS swarm technology and autonomous operations
- UAS cybersecurity and secure communications

- UAS logistics and sustainment

**Contracting Impact:**

### 6. Outbound Investment Controls Expansion ‚ö†Ô∏è **NEW RESTRICTIONS**

**What It Is:**
- Expands Treasury's outbound investment regime

- Tightens measures to prevent circumvention by Chinese military companies

**Affected Agencies:**
- ‚úÖ **Department of Defense** (all components)
- ‚úÖ **Department of Commerce** - Export controls
- ‚úÖ **Department of State** - International security
- ‚úÖ **Missile Defense Agency (MDA)** - Hypersonic defense
- ‚úÖ **Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA)** - Hypersonic research
- ‚úÖ **All Agencies with International Collaborations**

**New Pain Points to Add:**
- Outbound investment compliance and monitoring
- Hypersonic technology export control compliance
- Enhanced due diligence for international partnerships
- Supply chain risk assessment for restricted countries
- Compliance systems for expanded country restrictions
- Technology transfer controls and monitoring
- International collaboration risk management

**Contracting Impact:**
- Compliance consulting and monitoring services

- Technology transfer control systems

### 8. Cost Accounting Standards (CAS) Threshold Changes ‚ö†Ô∏è **PROCESS CHANGE**

**What It Is:**
- CAS applicability threshold raised from $2M to $35M
- Full CAS compliance threshold raised from $50M to $100M
- Cost or pricing data threshold raised from $2M to $10M (for contracts after June 30, 2026)

**Affected Agencies:**
- ‚úÖ **All Contracting Agencies**
- ‚úÖ **Defense Contract Management Agency (DCMA)** - Contract administration
- ‚úÖ **Defense Contract Audit Agency (DCAA)** - Audit processes
- ‚úÖ **Army Contracting Command** - Contract processing
- ‚úÖ **All Service Contracting Commands**

**New Pain Points to Add:**
- CAS threshold change implementation and training
- Contract pricing and cost data requirements updates
- Small business contract compliance (reduced CAS burden)
- Contract administration system updates for new thresholds
- Workforce training on new CAS and pricing thresholds
- Process optimization for reduced CAS coverage

**Contracting Impact:**
- Training programs for new thresholds
- Contract administration system updates
- Compliance consulting for threshold changes
